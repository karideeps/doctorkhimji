About

Dr. Hussain Khimji is passionate about helping people lead happy, fulfilling lives through his practice in Lifestyle Medicine and Obesity Medicine. Dr. Khimji values self-discovery, self-actualization, and assisting individuals with a sustainable, healthy lifestyle and behaviour change, emphasising the journey more than the destination.

Dr. Khimji obtained his medical degree in Dublin, Ireland, at the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland. He is a Canadian board certified in Family Medicine, and American board certified in Lifestyle Medicine. Aligned with his curiosity and passion for lifelong learning, he is preparing for his American board certification. He has previously worked in rural Medicine (Primary Care Practice, Hospitalist Medicine, Emergency Medicine) and urban Family Medicine. He has an ongoing role as an Urgent Care physician, which reminds him of the importance of disease prevention and reversal.

Outside of clinical practice, you can find Dr. Khimji trying to follow the advice he shares. He enjoys walking, cycling, weightlifting, and time outdoors in nature. The remainder of his leisure time involves reading, travelling, and spending quality time with family and friends!
